# Icon + Thumbnail Brief

A brief for the art **you** source/commission. This is direction, not the art.

## The job of each asset
- **Icon (512×512):** win the click in a tiny square. ONE big expressive brainrot face + a clear emotion (greedy grin / shock). Bold rim light, high saturation, readable at 50px. No text (or one word max). This is your most important art — iterate on it most.
- **Thumbnails (1920×1080, ship 3):** sell the fantasy + the hook.
  1. **The Steal:** one character yanking a glowing rare brainrot off another's base, victim reacting. Big arrow / "STEAL!" energy. This is the core loop — make it the primary.
  2. **The Flex / Rarity:** a wall of brainrots ascending from grey Common to glowing rainbow SECRET, with a huge "+$" number. Conveys progression + chase.
  3. **The Codes / Update hook:** a brainrot holding a "CODE: LAUNCH" sign or a "2x" burst. Tells searchers there are codes (retention).

## Must convey (rank order)
1. **Chaos / steal energy** — motion, reaction faces, "you can take their stuff."
2. **Rarity / flex** — glowing rare unit, big numbers, "imagine owning that."
3. **A clear hook** — one readable idea per image; a kid scrolling on a phone decides in <1s.

## Do
- Faces + eyes (Roblox CTR loves expressive faces). Strong silhouette. Saturated, high-contrast. Consistent style across all 3.
- Match the icon's hero brainrot to one a player actually gets early.

## Don't
- Cram 10 things in. No tiny text. No muddy lighting. No stolen/IP-risky character art (the roster names are placeholders/data — keep art original to avoid takedowns).
- Don't show UI screenshots as thumbnails — they don't sell.

## Counts / sizes
- Icon: 512×512 PNG.
- Thumbnails: 1920×1080 PNG, upload at least 3 (you can add more and reorder; #1 shows biggest).
- Refresh the icon if CTR is low — treat it as A/B testable.
